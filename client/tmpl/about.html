About

I'M STEPHANIE FOO

I'm a journalist, an artist, and above all, a storyteller.

I'm a producer at the public radio show This American Life. Basically, what that means is I make radio stories.

Prior to this sweet gig, I was a producer at the NPR show Snap Judgment. I started as an intern just a couple months after the show launched and over the course of the next four years, I produced over 180 stories for them and helped shape their signature style and sound. In 2012, I also hosted my own podcast, Stagedive, where I had musicians tell me personal narratives, which I then scored with their own music.

I care deeply about diversity in journalism. My desire to make public radio truly public informs all of the stories I tell, and the way I tell them. I enjoy educating radio hatchlings and speaking out against a singular public radio voice. I aim to encourage people of all backgrounds to join me in telling vibrant, impactful stories.
